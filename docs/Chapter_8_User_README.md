# User Guide: Chapter 8 Features
## Enhancing Your Security, Chat Experience, and Learning Pages

Welcome to **Chapter 8** of the NIYUVA App! In this update, we have made the application much safer, resolved issues where pages were cut off, and made the learning cards look absolutely beautiful.

---

## 🌟 What's New?

### 1. Smart Lockout Security
To protect your sensitive logs and conversations from unauthorized access, we have introduced a security lock timer. If someone enters a wrong PIN repeatedly, the app will block further entries for a period:
*   **5 incorrect entries**: 30 seconds block.
*   **10 incorrect entries**: 5 minutes block.
*   **20 incorrect entries**: 15 minutes block.
Even if someone closes and re-opens the app, the lock timer continues to count down!

### 2. Premium "Body" Learning Cards
The cards under the **Body** tab have been redesigned with a gorgeous soft vertical color gradient, sleek borders, dynamic badges, and circular boxes for icons. They also press down gently with a satisfying bounce animation when you tap them.

### 3. Smooth Chat Scrolling (Saarthi)
When you return to your chat tab, **Saarthi** will automatically and instantly slide down to show you the latest messages. No more scrolling down manually to read the newest replies!

### 4. No More Cutoff Texts
We fixed the issue where the text of long articles or lists would disappear behind the bottom navigation buttons. Now, you can scroll easily to the very bottom of every page.

## 📱 How to Use These Screens

### A. Entering Your PIN
1.  Open the app. You will see the **Apna PIN daalo** screen.
2.  Press the numbers on the keypad to enter your 4-digit PIN.
3.  If you make a mistake, use the backspace icon (arrow button on the bottom right) to delete the last number.
4.  If the PIN is correct, you are taken to the home screen.
5.  If you enter a wrong PIN 5 times:
    *   A **Security Alert** window will pop up warning you about the failed attempts.
    *   The app will lock for 30 seconds, showing a countdown timer.
    *   You can select **Security Question Try Karo** to answer your secret question and reset your PIN immediately.

### B. Resetting a Forgotten PIN
If you forget your PIN, don't worry! You don't have to lose your data:
1.  On the PIN screen, tap **PIN bhool gayi?** at the bottom of the page.
2.  A security sheet will slide up from the bottom of the screen.
3.  Answer the secret security question you chose during your registration.
4.  If the answer is correct, you can set a brand new 4-digit PIN and log back in.
5.  If you cannot remember the security question, you can choose to reset the app completely (this deletes all local logs and resets the setup).

### C. Reading Learning Articles (Body Tab)
1.  Tap the **Body** tab in the bottom menu.
2.  Browse the cards. They are sorted by category and colored to match their topic (e.g. green for Cycle, purple for Hormones, blue for Hygiene).
3.  Tap a card to open it. Note the smooth hover animation!
4.  You can read the entire article comfortably. Use your finger to scroll all the way down—none of the text will be hidden behind the bottom navigation buttons.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

### The app says "Security Lockout". How can I open it?
This happens when too many wrong PIN attempts are recorded.
*   **Option 1**: Wait for the timer shown on the screen to reach zero, then enter your correct PIN.
*   **Option 2**: Click **PIN bhool gayi?** to answer your security question. Successful verification resets the lock immediately.

### Why won't the keypad respond to my taps?
During an active lockout timer, the keypad is disabled to prevent unauthorized attempts. The keys will start working again as soon as the countdown timer disappears.
