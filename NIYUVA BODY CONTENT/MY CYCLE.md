## 💬 Pehle Ek Baat

Teri body har mahine ek kaam karti hai — quietly, bina kisi ko bataye — aur woh kaam hai **khud ko tayaar karna.** Kisi ke liye nahi, sirf apne liye. Yeh cycle sirf "periods aana" nahi hai. Yeh teri body ka ek poora rhythm hai — hormones, energy, mood, sab kuch ek saath chalte hain. Aur jab tu samajhti hai yeh rhythm ko, toh life thodi zyada *apni* lagti hai.

Toh chal, samajhte hain — dost ki tarah, science ke saath.

---

## 🔄 Cycle Kya Hoti Hai? (Pehle Basics)

Teri menstrual cycle **Day 1 se shuru hoti hai** — woh pehla din jab period aata hai — aur **agli period ke aane tak chalti hai.**

**Average cycle:** 28 din  
**Normal range:** 21 se 35 din  
**Teenagers ke liye:** 21 se 45 din bhi normal hai — body abhi settle ho rahi hai!

> 💡 **Yaad rakh:** Har ladki ki cycle alag hoti hai. 28 din "standard" hai, par agar teri 32 din ki hai — woh bhi bilkul theek hai. Tu wrong nahi hai, tu just *tu* hai.

## 🌙 Cycle Ke 4 Phases — Teri 4 Vibes

Soch isko aise — jaise ek mahine mein teri body **4 alag moods** mein hoti hai. Sach mein hormones aisa hi karte hain!

---

### 🔴 Phase 1 — Menstruation (Din 1–5)
**"Rest wali vibe"**

Yahi woh time hai jab period aata hai. Teri uterus ki lining — jo ek cozy bed ki tarah thi ek potential baby ke liye — ab shed hoti hai kyunki pregnancy nahi hui. Yeh blood nahi sirf, **blood + tissue + mucus** hota hai. Ekdum natural.

**Body mein kya ho raha hai:**
- Estrogen aur Progesterone — dono low hain
- Isliye thakaan, cramps, aur low mood feel hoti hai
- Yeh weakness nahi hai — yeh literally teri body ka hard work hai

**Flow kitna:** 3 se 7 din, light se heavy
**Cramps kyun:** Uterus contract karti hai lining bahar karne ke liye — prostaglandins naam ke chemicals iske zimmedar hain

> 🫂 **Dost wali baat:** Agar cramps bahut zyada hain ki school/college miss ho, toh yeh "normal" nahi hai — doctor se baat karna zaroori hai. Thoda cramp = normal. Rona aur bed se na uth paana = check kara.

---

### 🌱 Phase 2 — Follicular Phase (Din 1–13)
**"Slowly glow up wali vibe"**

Yeh phase actually period ke saath hi shuru hoti hai aur ovulation tak chalti hai. Is time mein brain ek hormone bhejta hai — **FSH (Follicle Stimulating Hormone)** — jo ovaries ko signal karta hai: *"Ek egg tayaar karo!"*

**Body mein kya ho raha hai:**
- Estrogen dheere dheere badhta hai
- Estrogen = serotonin (feel-good hormone) bhi badhta hai
- Energy wapas aati hai, skin thodi better lagti hai
- Mind clearer hota hai, padhai ya koi kaam zyada acha lagta hai

> ✨ **Fun fact:** Is phase mein tu genuinely zyada confident aur social feel karti hai — yeh attitude nahi, yeh science hai!

---

### ⚡ Phase 3 — Ovulation (Din 14 ke aaspaas)
**"Main character energy"**

Yeh cycle ka peak moment hai. **LH (Luteinizing Hormone)** ek surge karta hai aur ek mature egg ovary se release hoti hai — isko **ovulation** kehte hain. Yeh egg fallopian tube mein travel karti hai.

**Body mein kya ho raha hai:**
- Estrogen apne highest point par hota hai
- Mood best hota hai — confident, energetic, social
- Yeh sirf 12–24 ghante rehta hai (egg ka window)

> 💡 **Important:** Agar sperm available ho is window mein — pregnancy ho sakti hai. Isliye cycle samajhna apne health ke liye zaroor hai, chahe koi bhi reason ho.

---

### 🌊 Phase 4 — Luteal Phase (Din 15–28)
**"Cozy + moody + self-care wali vibe"**

Ovulation ke baad, jo follicle tha woh **corpus luteum** ban jaata hai aur **Progesterone** release karna shuru karta hai. Yeh hormone body ko bolti hai — "shayad pregnancy hui hai, tayaar raho."

Agar pregnancy nahi hoti, toh din 25–28 ke aaspaas dono — estrogen aur progesterone — dono girne lagte hain. Aur tab aata hai woh famous...

**PMS (Premenstrual Syndrome)**

**Kya ho sakta hai:**
- Mood swings — ek minute theek, ek minute rona
- Bloating — pet heavy lagta hai
- Cravings — chocolate, namkeen, kuch bhi
- Acne — kyunki hormones skin ko bhi affect karte hain
- Thakaan — progesterone ek mild sedative ki tarah kaam karta hai

> 🫶 **Yaad rakh:** PMS real hai. Yeh "drama" nahi hai. Hormones literally teri brain chemistry ko affect karte hain — serotonin aur dopamine dono fluctuate karte hain. Tu kuch zyada sensitive nahi hai — teri body ek bada hormonal shift le rahi hai.

## 🧠 Hormones Ka Short Map

| Hormone | Kaam kya karta hai | Mood pe asar |
|---|---|---|
| **Estrogen** | Egg tayaar karna, lining banana | Energy ↑, mood ↑, confidence ↑ |
| **Progesterone** | Pregnancy ke liye tayaar rehna | Calming but thoda sedating |
| **FSH** | Egg develop karna | — |
| **LH** | Ovulation trigger karna | — |
| **Serotonin** | Feel-good neurotransmitter | Estrogen ke saath badhta hai |

## 🚨 Kab Doctor Ke Paas Jaana Chahiye?

Yeh symptoms "normal" nahi hain — inhe ignore mat karo:

- ❌ Period 7 din se zyada chale aur bahut heavy ho
- ❌ Cramps itne bure hon ki painkiller bhi kaam na kare
- ❌ 3 mahine tak period na aaye (aur pregnant nahi ho)
- ❌ Cycles hamesha 45 din se zyada ho teen ke baad bhi
- ❌ PMS itna severe ho ki daily life affect ho (yeh PMDD ho sakta hai)

> Apni body ki awaaz suno. Tu khud ko sabse behtar jaanti hai.

## 🎨 APP REPRESENTATION GUIDE

> *Yeh section developer/designer ke liye hai — har section ko app mein kaise dikhayein.*

---

### 🖼️ Visual Language

**Color Palette per Phase:**
- 🔴 Menstruation → Deep rose / warm red `#C2405A`
- 🌱 Follicular → Soft sage green `#7DBE8E`
- ⚡ Ovulation → Warm golden yellow `#F5C842`
- 🌊 Luteal → Soft lavender / muted blue `#A48DC1`

**Overall App Mood:** Warm, soft, NOT clinical. Think journaling app, not hospital chart.

---

### 📱 Screen-by-Screen Breakdown

#### 1. Home / Cycle Wheel Screen
- **Visual:** Circular cycle wheel divided into 4 color-coded phases
- Show current day highlighted with a soft glow
- Emoji + 1-line mood description per phase (e.g. "Main character energy ⚡")
- Tap on a phase → opens phase detail card

#### 2. Phase Detail Card
- Big phase emoji + phase name in Hinglish
- 3-line personal description (warm, first-person feel)
- Hormone bar — simple visual bars showing Estrogen / Progesterone levels (no numbers, just low/medium/high)
- "Aaj ke liye tip" — one self-care or lifestyle nudge

#### 3. Mood + Symptom Log
- Emoji-first logging (not checkboxes)
- Options: 😴 Thaka hua / 😊 Acha lag raha hai / 😤 Irritable / 🤧 Cramps / 💧 Bloating
- Simple, tap-to-select, no typing needed
- Shows weekly mood pattern as a soft wave graph

#### 4. Hormone Explainer (Learn Section)
- Card-style swipeable content
- One hormone per card — name, emoji, simple Hinglish line
- E.g. Estrogen card: "🌟 Yeh wala hormone teri energy aur confidence ke liye zimmedar hai. Jab yeh high hota hai, tu zyada glow karti hai!"
- Keep it conversational, no jargon

#### 5. Period Tracker / Calendar
- Soft color-coded calendar
- Phase color bleeds into each date gently
- Period days = red dots, ovulation = star icon
- "Agle period mein X din bache hain" — countdown in friendly language

#### 6. Alert / When to See a Doctor
- Gentle yellow warning card (NOT red — red = panic)
- Title: "Teri body kuch bol rahi hai 👂"
- List symptoms in plain Hinglish with soft icons
- CTA: "Doctor se baat karo — yeh brave hona hai, weakness nahi"

---

- Phase transition = soft fade animation (like seasons changing)

- Streak = soft star fill, not aggressive gamification
