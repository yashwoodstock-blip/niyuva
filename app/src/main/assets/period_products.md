## 🟥 PART 1 — SANITARY PADS

### Yeh Hota Kya Hai?

Pad ek absorbent layer hoti hai jo teri underwear ke andar chipakti hai — **bahar se blood absorb karti hai.** India mein sabse commonly used product — 87% se zyada women yahi use karti hain.

---

### Pad Ke Types — Kaunsa Kab?

| Type | Kab Use Karo | Size |
|---|---|---|
| Panty liner | Spotting ya light discharge | Bahut thin, chhoti |
| Regular | Normal flow din | Medium |
| Heavy / XL | Heavy flow din | Bada, zyada absorption |
| Overnight / XXL | Raat ko sone ke liye | Lamba, extra coverage |
| Ultra thin | Active din, gym | Thin par absorbent |
| Wings wala | Movement mein extra security | Side wings fold karke chipakti hain |

> 💛 **Real baat:** Heavy flow ka pad light flow pe use karna = pad der tak nahi badlegi. Yeh wrong hai — bacteria grow karta hai. Flow ke hisaab se sahi size lo.

---

### Pad Kaise Lagaao — Step by Step

1. **Haath dhao** — pehle always
2. Pad ka wrapper kholao
3. **Adhesive strip** (neeche wali covering) hataao
4. Pad ko underwear ke **beech mein** chipkaao — seedha, tilt nahi
5. **Wings** hain toh — underwear ke neeche se fold karke chipkaao
6. Comfortable lage — shift nahi ho raha toh sahi hai

> 💡 **Tip:** Wings wale pad shorts ya active wear mein better rehte hain — shift nahi hote.

---

### Pad Kab Change Karo?

⏰ **Har 4-6 ghante** — chahe flow light ho ya heavy  
⏰ **Agar pad wet feel ho** — turant change karo, timer mat dekho  
⏰ **Exercise ke baad** — sweat + blood = bacteria fast grow karta hai  
⏰ **Raat ko** — sone se pehle fresh pad lagao, uthke turant change karo  

> ⚠️ **8 ghante se zyada KABHI NAHI** — infection, rash, aur bacteria ka risk bahut badh jaata hai.

---

### Pad Rash — Kyun Hoti Hai, Kaise Rokein

**Wajah:**
- Zyada der tak ek pad rehna
- Synthetic/plastic-based pad → heat trap karta hai
- Scented pad → chemicals sensitive skin irritate karte hain
- Friction → thighs aur pad ke beech chafing

**Kaise Bachein:**
✅ **Cotton-top** ya **organic cotton pads** choose karo — skin pe gentle  
✅ Fragrance-free pads — "fresh scent" wale pads actual irritant hain  
✅ Sahi time pe change karo  
✅ Pad change karte waqt area ko warm paani se clean karo aur pat dry karo  
✅ Agar rash ho gayi — **petroleum jelly (Vaseline)** thin layer se soothe karta hai  
✅ Zyada walk karna ho toh — thoda powder (unscented) thighs pe  

**India ke liye good brands:** Sirona, Carmesi, Nua (cotton-based, fragrance-free)

---

### Pad Dispose Kaise Karo

**Sahi tarika:**
1. Used pad ko **fold karo** — blood andar band ho jaye
2. **Wrap karo** — original wrapper mein ya tissue/newspaper mein
3. **Dustbin mein daalo** — dhakkan wala ho toh better
4. **Toilet mein flush mat karo** — pipes jam ho jaate hain aur environment ko nuksan

> 🌍 **Eco note:** Ek plastic pad 500-800 saal mein decompose hoti hai. Ek ladki apni life mein lagbhag 11,000-16,000 pads use karti hai. Isliye cotton ya biodegradable options socho.

## 🟡 PART 2 — TAMPONS

### Pehle — Myths Todo

❌ **"Tampon se virginity jaati hai"** — BILKUL GALAT. Virginity ek social concept hai, medical term nahi. Tampon hymen stretch kar sakta hai — tear nahi, destroy nahi. Aur hymen ka koi fixed shape nahi hota.  
❌ **"Andar kho jaata hai"** — Nahi jaata. Vagina ek closed space hai — cervix ke upar kuch nahi ja sakta.  
❌ **"Sirf sexual experience ke baad use kar sakte hain"** — Galat. Koi bhi use kar sakti hai.  
❌ **"Bahut dard hota hai insert karte waqt"** — Agar sahi karo toh bilkul feel nahi hota.  

---

### Tampon Kya Hota Hai?

Cotton ya rayon ka cylindrical piece jo **vagina ke andar jaata hai** aur blood andar hi absorb karta hai. Ek string bahar rehti hai — remove karne ke liye.

**Do types:**
- **Applicator wala** — plastic ya cardboard tube se insert karna easy hota hai — beginners ke liye better
- **Non-applicator** — finger se insert karte hain — practice lagti hai

---

### Tampon Kaise Use Karo — Step by Step

**Pehle ek baar padhna zaroori hai — panic mat karo, easy hai!**

**Step 1 — Prepare karo:**
- Haath achhe se dhao
- Comfortable flow wala din choose karo pehli baar ke liye — heavy flow days mein easier hota hai
- Sahi absorbency lo — apne flow ke liye

**Step 2 — Position lo:**
Koi bhi ek position try karo:
- Toilet pe baitho, legs thodi apart
- Ek paon toilet seat pe rakhke khadi ho
- Squat karo

**Step 3 — Relax karo:**
Yeh sabse important step hai. Tense hogi toh muscles tight hongi aur insert nahi hoga — ya dard karega. 2-3 deep breaths lo. Seriously.

**Step 4 — Insert karo (Applicator wala):**
1. Tampon ko outer tube ke beech mein pakdo
2. Tip ko vaginal opening pe rakhao — slight 45-degree angle — seedha nahi, thoda peeche ki taraf
3. Outer tube ko andar tak push karo gently
4. Inner tube se tampon ko andar dhakelo
5. Applicator bahar nikalo — string bahar rahegi
6. **Feel nahi hona chahiye** — agar feel ho raha hai toh aur andar karo ya reposition karo

**Correctly placed tampon bilkul feel nahi hota.** Agar feel ho raha hai — woh sahi position mein nahi hai, andar aur karo ya nikaalo aur try karo.

**Step 5 — Remove karo:**
- String ko gently pull karo — angle same rakhke
- Relax karo pehle — tense mat ho
- Slowly wiggle karte hue bahar nikalo
- Used tampon wrap karo aur dustbin mein — flush nahi

---

### Absorbency Guide

| Flow | Absorbency |
|---|---|
| Bahut light | Light / Slim |
| Normal | Regular |
| Heavy | Super |
| Bahut heavy | Super Plus |

> ⚠️ **Rule:** Hamesha **lowest possible absorbency** use karo jo tumhare flow ke liye kaam kare. Zyada absorbency = TSS risk zyada. Light flow pe Super tampon mat lo.

---

### ⚠️ TSS — Toxic Shock Syndrome — Sach Jaano, Dar Mat

**Yeh kya hai?**
Ek rare par serious bacterial infection — Staphylococcus aureus bacteria ke toxins se. TSS sirf tampon se nahi hota — kisi bhi vaginal product se ho sakta hai agar bahut der tak raha.

**Risk kab badhta hai:**
- Tampon 8 ghante se zyada andar raha
- Bahut high absorbency unnecessary use ki
- Periods ke beech mein tampon use kiya

**TSS ke signs — yeh ho toh turant tampon nikalo aur doctor ke paas jao:**
🚨 Sudden tez bukhar (102°F / 39°C se zyada)  
🚨 Sunburn jaisi rash (especially haath-paon pe)  
🚨 Ulti, dast  
🚨 Khade hote waqt chakkar ya behoshi  
🚨 Muscles mein dard  

**TSS rare hai — par real hai.** Rules follow karo aur panic nahi.

**Safe tampon use ke rules:**
✅ Har **4-8 ghante** mein change — 8 ghante **strict limit**  
✅ Raat ko **pad** use karo — tampon nahi  
✅ Haath dhao pehle  
✅ Lowest absorbency jo kaam kare  
✅ Period ke beech mein kabhi nahi

## 🟢 PART 3 — MENSTRUAL CUP

### Yeh Kya Hai?

Medical-grade silicone ka ek chhota, flexible cup — vagina ke andar jaata hai aur blood **collect** karta hai (absorb nahi). Ek cup **5-10 saal** tak chalta hai.

---

### Cup Kyun? — Real Reasons

💰 **Pocket ke liye:** Ek baar ₹300-800 invest karo — saalon mein pads pe hazar rupaye bachte hain  
🌍 **Planet ke liye:** Zero waste — plastic pads ki jagah  
⏰ **Time ke liye:** 6-12 ghante tak kuch nahi — school, college, travel — tension free  
🏊 **Active life ke liye:** Swimming, gym, dance — sab kuch  
🌸 **Skin ke liye:** No rash, no chemicals, no friction  

---

### Pehle Use Karne Se Pehle — Sterilize Karo

**Pehli baar aur har period ke shuru mein:**
1. Ek dedicated pot mein paani ubhalo
2. Cup daalo — pot ki side ya bottom se touch nahi karna chahiye (tongs use karo)
3. **5-7 minute** ubhalo
4. Cool hone do — phir use karo

**Period ke dauraan (har bar change karte waqt):**
- Sirf warm paani aur mild unscented soap se wash karo
- Har 6-12 ghante mein — boil ki zaroorat nahi

---

### Cup Kaise Insert Karo — 3 Folding Methods

**Pehle haath dhao!**

#### Method 1 — C-Fold (Beginners ke liye easiest)
Cup ko flat karo, phir C shape mein fold karo — jaise ek half-moon

#### Method 2 — Punch-Down Fold
Apni index finger se rim ke ek point ko andar push karo — cup chhota aur tight fold banta hai — insert karna easy

#### Method 3 — 7-Fold
Cup ko flat karo, ek side ko fold karke 7 ki shape banao

> 💡 **Tip:** Pehle C-fold try karo. Kuch logon ko punch-down better lagta hai. Experiment karo!

---

### Insert Kaise Karo

1. Comfortable position lo — toilet pe baitho, squat ya ek paon upar
2. Folded cup ko gently vaginal opening pe rakhao
3. **Relax karo** — seriously, yeh zyada important hai than you think
4. Rim first, thoda angle pe andar push karo
5. Cup andar jaane ke baad — **ek baar ghuma do** (rotate) — yeh seal create karta hai
6. Stem bahar nahi nikalna chahiye — par andar bhi itna deep nahi ki pakad na sako

**Sahi seal ka test:** Gently stem ko pull karo — resistance feel hona chahiye (suction). Kuch nahi feel hua — cup ne seal nahi kiya. Ek baar rotate karo ya nikaalo aur retry.

---

### Remove Kaise Karo

1. Haath dhao
2. Toilet pe baitho ya squat karo
3. **Stem ko dhundo** — teri finger se
4. Stem ke upar grip rings tak jao
5. Base ko **pinch karo** — yeh suction/seal tod deta hai
6. **Seal tode bina mat khicho** — dard hoga!
7. Seal tod ke — gently wiggle karte hue nikaalo
8. Blood toilet mein daalo, cup rinse karo, reinsert

> 💛 **Pehli baar dar laga?** Normal hai. Pehli koshish mein nahi hua? Normal hai. Cup ek skill hai — ek cycle mein ho jaata hai. YouTube pe video dekh — visual help bahut kaam aati hai.

---

### Public Bathroom Mein Kya Karo?

Yeh real problem hai — par solution hai:

**Option 1:** Bottle mein paani le jao — cup rinse karo sink pe jaane se pehle  
**Option 2:** Tissue se wipe karo aur reinsert — ghar aake proper wash  
**Option 3:** Single-occupancy bathroom use karo jahan sink ho  

---

### Cup Store Kaise Karo?

- **Breathable cotton pouch** mein — box mein aata hai wahi
- **Airtight container mein nahi** — moisture trap hoti hai
- **Direct sunlight se dur** — silicone degrade hoti hai
- Staining normal hai — sun mein 24 ghante rakh ke remove ho sakti hai

**India mein available cups:** Sirona, She Cup, Shecup, Stonesoup, Rustic Art (₹300-800 range)

## 🟣 PART 4 — PERIOD UNDERWEAR

### Yeh Kya Hai?

Special underwear jisme **built-in absorbent layers** hoti hain — bilkul normal underwear jaisi dikhti hai, feel hoti hai — par andar se period protection hai.

**3 Layers kaam karti hain:**
1. **Top layer** — moisture wicks away, skin dry rehti hai
2. **Middle layer** — blood absorb karke lock karta hai
3. **Bottom layer** — waterproof, kapde tak nahi pahunchta

---

### Period Underwear Kiske Liye Best Hai?

✅ **Beginners** — pehla period? yeh sabse easy aur non-intimidating option  
✅ **Raat ke liye** — cup ya pad se better, koi leak tension nahi  
✅ **Light days** — akele use kar sakti hai  
✅ **Backup** — cup ya tampon ke saath extra security  
✅ **Sensitive skin** — rash-prone girls ke liye pad se much better  
✅ **Active days** — gym, sports, school — comfortable  

---

### Kab Change Karo?

- **6-10 ghante** — flow ke hisaab se
- **Heavy flow** pe 4-6 ghante better
- **Raat ke liye** — ek pair kaafi hoti hai

---

### Wash Kaise Karo?

1. **Pehle cold paani se rinse karo** — hot paani blood set kar deta hai aur dhona mushkil hota hai
2. **Machine wash** — delicate cycle, 30°C ya thanda paani
3. **Mesh laundry bag** use karo — fabric protect hota hai
4. **Fabric softener mat** — absorbency khatam hoti hai
5. **Bleach kabhi nahi**
6. **Air dry** — dryer mein mat — waterproof layer kharab hoti hai

**India mein brands:** D'chica, SuperBottoms, Mahina, MyAdira, Avni (₹400-900 per pair)

> 💡 **Kitne pair chahiye?** 3-5 pair ek cycle ke liye — washing frequency pe depend karta hai.

## 🔵 PART 5 — CLOTH PADS

### Yeh Kya Hai?

Fabric se bane reusable pads — exact waise kaam karte hain jaise disposable pad — par wash karke reuse karte hain. **2-5 saal** chalte hain.

**Materials:** Cotton, bamboo, flannel — breathable aur skin-friendly

---

### Use Kaise Karo?

Exactly waise jaise disposable pad — underwear mein snappers ya wings se secure karo. Change karo har 4-6 ghante.

---

### Wash Kaise Karo?

1. Use hone ke baad **cold paani mein soak karo** pehle
2. Haath se ya machine mein dhoo — mild detergent
3. Hot paani avoid karo — blood set ho jaata hai
4. **Dhoop mein sukhaao** — natural sanitizer

> 💛 **India ke liye reality:** Cloth pads ki washing mein time lagta hai — par agar ghar mein comfortable feel ho toh yeh ekdum sustainable option hai. Starter kit se shuru karo — 2-3 pads try karo.

**India mein brands:** FabPad, Saathi, Anandi (₹150-350 per pad)

## 🎯 PART 7 — TERE LIYE KYA SAHI HAI?

### Choose Karo Apni Vibe Se:

**"Main pehli baar use kar rahi hoon / bohot simple chahiye"**
→ 🟥 **Pad** — Start karo simple se. Organic/cotton choose karo.

**"Period pe school/college/travel — tension free chahiye"**
→ 🟢 **Cup** — Ek baar seekho, life easy ho jaati hai. Ya 🟡 **Tampon** agar comfortable ho.

**"Raat ko tension nahi chahiye, uthke change nahi karna"**
→ 🟢 **Cup** ya 🟣 **Period Underwear** — dono raat ke liye best

**"Swimming / Sports / Active life"**
→ 🟢 **Cup** ya 🟡 **Tampon** — only internal options work

**"Skin sensitive hai, pad se rash hoti hai"**
→ 🟣 **Period Underwear** ya 🔵 **Cloth Pad** — no chemicals, no friction

**"Planet ki care karti hoon, plastic nahi chahiye"**
→ 🟢 **Cup** + 🟣 **Period Underwear** + 🔵 **Cloth Pad** — yeh combo best hai

**"Budget tight hai"**
→ Short term: 🟥 **Regular Pad** | Long term: 🟢 **Cup** ya 🔵 **Cloth Pad** — invest once, save for years

## 🚨 PART 8 — EMERGENCY PERIOD KIT

Teri bag mein hamesha hona chahiye — surprise period kisi ko bhi aa sakti hai!

**Basic Kit:**
```
🩸 2-3 pads ya tampons (apne preferred product)
💊 1-2 pain reliever (ibuprofen — cramps ke liye)
🩲 1 extra underwear (seriously)
🧻 Kuch tissues
🤲 Small hand sanitizer
🫙 Small zip-lock bag (disposal ke liye)
```

**Agar cup use karti ho:**
```
➕ Backup pad (pehle ek-do cycles mein)
➕ Small water bottle
```

> 💛 **Period pouch tip:** Ek chhoti sa cute pouch choose karo — sab isme rakhna. Bag change karte waqt pouch transfer karo — sab saath rehta hai.

## 🧺 PART 9 — DISPOSE KAISE KARO — SAHI TARIKA

### Pads:
1. Blood wali side ko andar fold karo
2. Side wings se wrap karo
3. Wrapper ya tissue mein wrap karo
4. **Dustbin mein** — dhakkan wale mein
5. ❌ Toilet mein nahi — pipes jam

### Tampons:
1. String se gently pull karo
2. Tissue mein wrap karo
3. **Dustbin mein** — toilet nahi
4. Applicator bhi dustbin mein

### Cup:
- Blood toilet mein
- Cup wash karo
- Reuse ✅

### Period Underwear / Cloth Pads:
- Pehle cold rinse
- Phir wash
- Reuse ✅

### Public Toilet Mein:
- Pad/tampon — wrap karke dustbin mein (almost sab public bathrooms mein dustbin hoti hai)
- Agar nahi hai — wrapper mein wrap karo, bag mein rakhho, bahar dustbin use karo
- ❌ Flush nahi — kabhi nahi — chahe product "flushable" likha ho

## 🌸 LAST BAAT

Period product choose karna ek personal journey hai. Jo teri saheli use karti hai woh tere liye perfect nahi ho sakta — aur yeh theek hai.

Ek product se start karo. Comfortable feel hone par dusra try karo. Koi galat answer nahi hai — bas tera answer hai.

Aur agar kuch bhi unclear ho, dard ho, ya kuch ajeeb lage — doctor se baat karo. Yeh sab normal conversations hain. Tu deserve karti hai proper care. 🩸
